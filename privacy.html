Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

InvolveUS (“the App”) is a tool designed to help users identify their elected representatives, track legislation, and stay informed about civic activity at the federal and state levels. We take privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how that information is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

The App allows you to enter the following information:

  • Address (street, city, state, and ZIP code)
  • Legislative interests (categories you select)
  • Saved bills (bills you choose to bookmark)

This information is used only on your device to deliver personalized results such as:

  • Identifying your correct representatives
  • Displaying local and federal legislation
  • Filtering bills by your chosen interests

1.2 No Accounts, No Login

The App does not require you to create an account. We do not store user profiles, authentication data, or passwords.

2. How Your Information Is Stored

All personal data (your address, interests, and saved bills) is stored locally on your device only using secure operating-system–level storage (AsyncStorage). We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to our own servers.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is deleted from your device.

3. How Your Information Is Shared

3.1 No Sale or Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

3.2 Transmission to Government Data Providers

To retrieve accurate representative and legislative information, the App may transmit portions of your address or ZIP code to third-party, publicly accessible government data providers, such as:

  • Congress.gov (Library of Congress)
  • OpenStates.org

These requests are used solely to look up:

  • Your federal and state representatives
  • Federal and state legislation relevant to your state or district

No additional personal data is transmitted beyond what is necessary to perform these lookups.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that information has been collected from a minor, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Third-Party Links

The App may include links to external government websites (e.g., representatives' official pages, bill text, or legislative portals). These websites are operated independently of InvolveUS. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

6. Data Security

Because all personal data remains on your device, security is primarily provided by your device’s operating system. The App does not transmit or store your personal information on our servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted at the same URL as this page, and the “Last Updated” date will reflect the most recent revision.
